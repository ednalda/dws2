Hours and Directions

Open daily from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. with the last admission ticket being sold at 4:15 p.m. The Boyton Beach Zoo is closed only on Thanksgiving and Christmas Days. 6783 Powerline Rd. Boynton Beach, FL 33456. Take 95 North exit 45/. 45 minutes from Miami.

Zoo Events

The Boynton Beach Zoo is known throughout South Florida for its stellar family festivals, children's education programs, holiday breakfasts, keeper talks and wildlife shows - please join us for some (or all) of them! We are waiting you and your group!

Education

Do your children belong in the zoo? We offer a variety of programs perfect for creating family memories. Register your children for zoo camp or for an exciting summer experience, attend a children’s workshop together, or even spend the night at the Zoo!

Group Events

Children who love animals will love a birthday party at the Boynton Beach Zoo! Zoo parties are safe, educational and a "roaring" good time for kids of all ages! Note: Birthday parties must have 20 paying guests to reserve your date. Welcome!

Visit the Zoo

Tucked away on the shore of beautiful Baker Lake in the heart of the Zoo, the Tropics Café is a pleasant hideaway offering full-service dining in a unique air-conditioned facility complete with an Amazon Basin theme. Restaurant seating is available 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. daily.For those seeking light refreshment, our Cool Waters Café, Tropical Concession* and Dippin’ Dots Stands offer cold drinks, novelty ice cream treats, hot dogs, and more.

Become a Member

Annual Membership Privileges

  • Unlimited free daytime admission to the Palm Beach Zoo for all those who qualify under your membership category;
  • Discounted admission to special evening events such as Safari Nights.
  • Discounted admission to over 140 zoos, aquariums, and museums nationwide;
  • 10% discount in the zoo's full-service Tropics Café, Tropics Concessions Area, Cool Waters Café and Dippin' Dots Stand, Amazon Marketplace and Jungle Traders Gift Shop;
  • Subscriptions to the monthly e-newsletter, You Belong in the Zoo and the online publication, Palm Beach Zoo Magazine;
  • Invitations to Members-Only events and exhibit openings;
  • Discounted registration for all zoo programs, including Holiday Breakfast Programs, Home School Classes, Zoo Camp, and Educational and Family Workshops;
  • Opportunities to assist and support your Palm Beach Zoo in worldwide and local conservation efforts such as our Native Wildlife Rehabilitation Programs and the Species Survival Plans (SSP) operated in cooperation with the Association of Zoos and Aquariums (AZA).
